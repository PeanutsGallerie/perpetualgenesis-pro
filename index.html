Help & About

This tool is designed to help you plan indoor seed starting and outdoor transplant timing based on your local frost dates and growing method. It is built for practical use â€” not perfection.

Calculator Tab

The core of the tool. Use it to:

  • Select season (Spring or Fall)
  • Choose crop category
  • Pick growing method (Outdoor, Row Covers, or Greenhouse)
  • Enter your last spring or first fall frost date (MM/DD/YYYY)
  • Click Calculate Dates to fill in personalized start/transplant/harvest windows
  • Use Reset to clear all calculated dates and the frost date field
  • Click + Add on any crop row to save it to My Garden

Dates are estimates based on standard horticultural data adjusted for your method.

My Garden Tab

Your personal dashboard. Features:

  • Multiple plans (create, rename, reorder, delete)
  • Each added crop shows its original settings and calculated dates
  • Track progress: Seeds Started â€¢ Transplanted â€¢ Harvested
  • Add personal notes per planting
  • Remove individual plantings or clear everything

Timeline Tab

Visual timeline of your growing season and harvest flow:

  • Progress markers appear when you mark steps complete in My Garden
  • Succession tips are highlighted below for continuous harvest crops
